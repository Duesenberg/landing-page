Enlightening facts about pears
There are over 3000 varieties of pears worldwide. The pear dynasty runs as deep as you can see.
Pears are one of the few fruits that have an extensive history, reaching back to about 1000 B.C. Basically as old as dinosaurs.

Pears used to be called “butter fruit” for its soft, butter-like texture. Unlike apples, who make doctors go away. Why would you want your doctor to leave you?
Pears are a part of the rose family. Basically fruit royalty.

Not many fruits have been called a “gift of the gods,” but according to the famous poet, Homer, that is just what a pear is.
